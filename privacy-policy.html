# FiberVault Privacy Policy FiberVault collects and stores information you choose to add to the app, such as stash details, project details, storage locations, and account information used for sign-in and syncing across devices. ## Information We Collect * Email address for account access * App content you create, such as yarn, fiber, projects, patterns, and storage entries * Photos you choose to upload * Basic technical information needed to operate the app ## How We Use Information We use your information to: * create and manage your account * save your data * sync your data across devices * improve app reliability and functionality ## Photo and Camera Access If permission is granted, FiberVault may request access to your photo library or camera so you can attach images to your stash, projects, or storage locations. ## Data Sharing FiberVault does not sell your personal information. Data is only used to provide app functionality and supporting services required to operate the app. ## Data Storage Your information may be stored securely using third-party infrastructure that supports account login and cloud sync. ## Your Choices You can choose what information you add to FiberVault. You may also stop using the app at any time. ## Contact For privacy questions, contact: spellspun.design@gmail.com
